About SatCli

SatCli is an website that shows people how climate change affects their area and what they can do to stop it in a fun, interactive

way. Our mission is to help people understand what they can do to stop climate change and how their environment could change for the worst.

Climate Change is a big problem in today's world and so we need to start taking action in order to prevent it from becoming even worse.

The features of this website include,
  • -a timeline that shows the past, present, and future of where you live.
  • -possible hazards that can happen in the future and how people can prevent it
  • -an analysis of what changed between selected years
And much more!

  • What is climate change?

    As the article here states, climate change in summary is the long-term alteration of temperature and typical weather patterns in a place. The cause of current climate change is largely human activity, like burning fossil fuels, like natural gas, oil, and coal. Burning these materials releases what are called greenhouse gases into Earth's atmosphere.
